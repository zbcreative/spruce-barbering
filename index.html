About Spruce Barbering

A Barbershop Built Around You

Spruce Barbering is a premium barbershop owned and operated by Amanda Bishop, located in O'Fallon, Missouri, inside the Phenix Salon Suites at 8654 Veterans Memorial Parkway. With over 12 years of professional barbering experience, Amanda delivers a curated grooming experience that starts and ends with the client.

Specializing in custom haircuts, straight razor fades, creative design work, steam towel face shaves, and professional waxing, Spruce Barbering serves clients throughout the St. Charles County area including O'Fallon, St. Peters, Lake St. Louis, Wentzville, Dardenne Prairie, and surrounding communities.

Every visit is an experience. A comfortable, inviting environment where skilled craftsmanship meets genuine hospitality. Whether you need a clean traditional cut or a bold new style, Amanda will make sure you leave looking and feeling your absolute best.
